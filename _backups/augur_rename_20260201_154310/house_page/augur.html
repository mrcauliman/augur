Local-first finance vault. Public wallets in. Monthly clarity out.

what we builtwhy it matters

DL is a vault-backed ledger for digital finances. You add public addresses. DL pulls history. DL writes events and snapshots to a local vault. Then DL generates monthly summaries you can actually use.

local-first your vault, your data
audit-ready JSONL event trail
multi-chain XRPL first, then more
no keys stored public addresses only
DL v1 scope
  • XRPL ingest: transfers in, transfers out, fees
  • Monthly report: totals and counts per account
  • Indexes: last_seen + tx_dedupe so re-runs stay clean
  • Observers for EVM, BTC, SOL exist as stubs, wired later
what this does that others don’t
  • Most trackers show a dashboard, then your data disappears into their platform
  • DL writes a vault you can version, back up, and prove
  • DL produces a clean monthly ledger output, not a “pretty chart” addiction loop
how it works 
1) Add public wallet address
2) Snapshot pulls balances + events
3) Vault writes:
   data/events
   data/snapshots
   data/indexes
4) Monthly job outputs:
   reports/monthly/YYYY-MM/summary.json
sample commands 
pnpm snapshot
pnpm monthly 2026-01

cat vault/sample_vault/reports/monthly/2026-01/summary.json
privacy rules
  • No seed phrases, no private keys, no signing inside DL v1
  • Premium features later can use Xaman sign-in, outside the vault
